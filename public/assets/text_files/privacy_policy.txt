<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="normal"><strong>What information do we collect?</strong></p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">We collect information from you when you register on our site, place an order, subscribe to our newsletter, respond to a survey or fill out a form.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">When ordering or registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter your name, e-mail address, mailing address, phone number or credit card information. You may, however, visit our site anonymously.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal"><strong>What do we use your information for?</strong></p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways:</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To personalize your experience - your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To improve our website - we continually strive to improve our website offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To improve customer service - your information helps us to more effectively respond to your customer service requests and support needs.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To process transactions - your information, whether public or private, other than for the express purpose of delivering the purchased product or service requested, we will not sell, exchange, transfer, or give your information to any other company for any reason without your consent.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To administer a contest, promotion, survey or other site feature.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">To send periodic emails - the email address you provide for order processing will only be used to send you information and updates pertaining to your order.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal"><strong>How do we protect your information?</strong></p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you place an order or enter, submit, or access your personal information.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">We offer the use of a secure server. All supplied sensitive/credit information is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database only to be accessible by those authorized with special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">After a transaction, your private information (credit cards, financials, etc.) will not be stored on our servers.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal"><strong>Do we use cookies?</strong></p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">Yes (Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your Web browser (if you allow) that enables the sites or service providers systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="normal">We use cookies to help us remember and process the items in your shopping cart, understand and save your preferences for future visits and compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction so that we can offer better site experiences and tools in the future.</p>
<p class="normal">&nbsp;</p>
